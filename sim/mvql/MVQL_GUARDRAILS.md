# MVQL Guardrails

## Prohibited Uses

MVQL must NOT be used to:

- Identify “risky” individuals
- Adjust caps for specific users
- Gate access
- Impose penalties
- Influence voting
- Drive capital allocation decisions centrally

---

## Allowed Uses

- Identify systemic fragility
- Trigger governance review discussion
- Inform voluntary coordination
- Provide transparency

---

## Adaptive Review Trigger

If any metric stays RED for >3 consecutive periods:
- Governance review required
- No automatic control actions allowed
- Any proposed change must pass certification

---

## Transparency Mandate

MVQL formulas:
- Must be publicly documented
- Must not change without version bump
- Must not be retroactively reweighted

---

## Final Principle

MVQL is a mirror.
Not a steering wheel.
