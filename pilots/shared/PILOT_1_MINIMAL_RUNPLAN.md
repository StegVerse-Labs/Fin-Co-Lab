# Pilot 1 — Minimal Run Plan
Duration: 4 Weeks
Epoch Length: 72 Hours
Total Epochs: 9

Goal:
Test guardrail clarity and reaction to controlled volatility in a small group.

No A/B split in this version.
Single cohort.

---

# Participant Setup

Each participant receives:

- 10,000 Vault SVU
- 500 Pocket SVU
- 250 SVU Vault→Pocket cap per epoch
- 100 SVU Essential cap per epoch

Vault:
Long-term storage. Cannot be spent directly.

Pocket:
Used for exchanges and essential spending.

---

# Weekly Structure

## Week 1 — Baseline

Epoch 1:
Normal operation.

Epoch 2:
Encourage task exchange.

Goal:
Observe natural behavior without pressure.

---

## Week 2 — Liquidity Pressure

Epoch 3:
Transfer clustering prompt:
“Increased liquidity activity observed.”

No rule change.

Goal:
Measure slope cap reactions.

---

## Week 3 — Essential Spike

Epoch 4:
Essential demand spike prompt.

Epoch 5:
Normal.

Goal:
Measure essential cap interpretation.

---

## Week 4 — Stabilization Event

Epoch 6:
Temporary Stabilization Mode (DSM) activated.
Duration: 2 epochs.

Epoch 7:
DSM active.

Epoch 8:
DSM auto-expires.

Epoch 9:
Final reflection epoch.

Goal:
Measure:
- Reaction to temporary constraint
- Trust in expiry
- Participation persistence

---

# Stress Injection Rules

- No additional surprise events.
- No mid-epoch rule changes.
- DSM auto-expires exactly as announced.
- All transparency snapshots posted weekly.

---

# Weekly MVQL Snapshot

Published once per week.

Includes:
- Essential Access Integrity
- Volatility Pressure Indicator
- Participation Activity Level

No individual metrics.
Aggregate only.

---

# End of Pilot

Participants receive:

- Summary of findings
- Guardrail clarity insights
- Noted revision targets
- Invitation to next version

Pilot ends automatically after Epoch 9.
